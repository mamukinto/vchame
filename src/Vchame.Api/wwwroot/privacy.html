Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

TL;DR: We don't collect personal information. Your data is anonymous. No accounts, no tracking, no ads.

What we store

vchame.ge stores the following data on our server:

  • Device ID — a random identifier generated by your browser (UUID). It cannot identify you personally.
  • Food counts — how many of each dish you tapped, per day.
  • Nickname — only if you choose to set one in the Friends feature. Optional.
  • Friend connections — which device IDs are linked as friends.

What we DON'T store

  • Name, email, phone number, or any personal contact info
  • Location data (location text you enter for share cards stays on your device only)
  • Photos (photos you add to share cards are processed on your device only — never uploaded)
  • IP addresses or browser fingerprints
  • Cookies (we use localStorage for functional purposes only)

Analytics & tracking

We do not use any analytics services (no Google Analytics, no tracking pixels, no third-party scripts). Zero tracking.

Third parties

We do not share any data with third parties. There are no ads.

Data storage

Data is stored on servers hosted by Railway and Neon (PostgreSQL database). Both are reputable infrastructure providers.

Data deletion

Since your data is tied to a random device ID (not an account), clearing your browser data effectively disconnects you from your stored data. If you want your data explicitly deleted from our servers, contact us.

Children

vchame.ge does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app collects no personal information from anyone.

Changes

If we change this policy, we'll update the date at the top. No prior data practices will be changed retroactively.
